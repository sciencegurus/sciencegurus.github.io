Get Involved
  • Volunteer to join the Science Gurus mailing list by emailing any of the officers. Our contact email address is contact@ScienceGurus.org
  • Science Gurus is driven by enthusiastic volunteers and a zero overhead organization such that no portion of a given donation is used to cover administrative costs. This means that 100% of all donations directly benefit the next generation scientists in India and United States.
  • Science Gurus is interested to partner with NGOs in India to conduct its activities related to Science education. Interested NGOs can write to us at contact@ScienceGurus.org
  • Science Gurus is a 501 (c) (3) charitable organization(Tax ID# 980443813) and donations made to Science Gurus are tax deductible under section 170 of the code.
