College Admissions Workshop FAQ
  • What is the College Admissions Workshop Program?

    The college admissions workshop is a guide for high school students and their families to help:

      Understand and appreciate the nuances, details of the admissions process
      Plan your journey of selecting a counselor with synergies to the student
      Discuss a plan that has worked, morph and get buy-in

  • What is the format of the program?

    The program consists of 1 - 1.5 hour long sessions specifically tailored towards freshmen/sophomores, juniors and seniors.

    • Who are the instructors?

    Shreya Kochar and Harpal Kochar, will be the main instructors. Shreya is a sophomore at Wellesley College, while her father Harpal Kochar works at Salesforce.

  • How many students will be accepted into the program?

    There is no limit on the number of participants.

  • Where will the workshops be held?

    Workshops will be held virtually via Zoom on Sundays during the months of September and October, 2020 between 3:00 - 4:00 PM PST.

  • Is there a fee for the workshops?

    It is free for current and past Science Gurus instructors, but there is a nominal fee of $25 per workshop to cover admin costs for everyone else.

  • What is the process for the workshops?

    After you register for the workshop, you will be emailed the link to pay for and complete the waiver the day before the workshop. The Zoom link will be emailed to all registered participants who complete the payment and waiver process. The link will be sent about 15 - 30 minutes before the workshop start time.
