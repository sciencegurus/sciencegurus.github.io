Science Speech Olympiad FAQ
  • What is the Science Speech Olympiad competition?

    Science Speech Olympiad is an annual competition for 7-9 grade students organized by Science Gurus to show case their knowledge of science through public speaking.

  • What is the format of the competition?

    * Individual students participate in one or multiple subjects in the competition.
    * The competition is open to 7-9 grade students from across the USA.
    * Students will speak on the assigned topics in Math, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
    * 2 topics in each subject have already been announced (Click here to view topics).
    * Student must speak on the assigned topic in the subjects they chose: Math, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
    * Actual topic for presentation in each subject will be revealed at the competition.
    * The time limit for presentation is 8 min. per student per subject & 2 min. Q&A session
       - 2 min. of Introduction
       - 5 min. of subject ,matter covering the points given in the topic
       - 1 min. of summary/conclusion
    * Judges may ask technical questions or clarifications at the end of the presentation.
    * Students may use a poster board to aid in their presentation.
